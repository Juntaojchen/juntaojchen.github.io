About Me

I am currently an MPhil candidate in Mathematics at Newcastle University, and I am privileged to be supervised by Prof. Chris Oates.

I received my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Chongqing University of Arts and Sciences, and participated in collaborative coursework with the University of North Carolina Wilmington.

My research interests include Probabilistic Machine Learning, Computational Statistics, and I am also passionate about Deep Learning.
